Our Story

Cooked with love.
Served like family.

Ana Restaurant brings the true flavors of the Dominican Republic to Baltimore's Highlandtown neighborhood on Eastern Ave. Every dish is made fresh, from scratch, with natural seasoning and recipes rooted in tradition.

We started with a simple idea: give this city food that feels like home. Whether you grew up on this food or you've never tried it — you belong at our table.

"Delicious fried fish, chicken, beef, rice, beans, tostones, and salad with a yummy dressing. Generous portions, authentic taste, and the music that transports diners to the Dominican Republic."
